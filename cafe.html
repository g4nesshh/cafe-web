Our Story

Crafted with
passion & precision

At Brûlée, every cup is a quiet ceremony. We source single-origin beans directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasting in small batches to coax out each bean's unique character.

Our baristas are trained in the art of balance — the interplay of extraction, temperature, and time that separates a good coffee from an unforgettable one.
